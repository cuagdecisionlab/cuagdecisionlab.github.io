Welcome to our homepage!


Clemson Agribusiness Decision Research Lab is led by Dr. Yefan Nian and Dr. Michael Vassalos. Our research lab is found in 2024 and is housed at Department of Agricultural Sciences, Clemson University.

Our lab’s research mainly covers people’s economic decision-making in agribusiness. In our lab, we conduct behavioral science and economic experiments to better understand how people form judgments and make decisions in the conext of agribusiness. We also conduct research to evaluate the implications of people’s decisions on the sucess of agribusiness.
